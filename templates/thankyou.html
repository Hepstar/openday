Survey and Demonstration Background:

SYMPACT is a joint research project between University of Bristol, University of Surrey and Guardian News and Media that is exploring the environmental impact of digital transformation.

This exhibit was developed by SYMPACT, in collaboration with KTH CESC, to demonstrate the energy consumed when delivering digital media over the Internet. It consists of an 'Energy Explorer' application designed to allow users to explore internet energy consumption, and a short survey to provide further research data for SYMPACT .

If you would like further information please contact us at info@sympact.org
